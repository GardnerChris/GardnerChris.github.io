Website Plan

Target-Audience

The target audience is perminent residents and visitors interested in information specific to each town. This includes single and married adults, teenagers and college students. Additionally, those that want to plan their outdoor activities and need weather infromation are also a releviant audience.

Persona

The Bronsons

The Bronsons are a small young family that enjoy bonding outdoors together with their dog rimshot. They enjoy picnics, going to parks, riding bicycles, having a place that can give them weather information specific to Preston is important to them.

Roger Young

Roger is a college student in Rexburg Idaho. Most of his time is spent indoors studying. Howeever, when he has spare time he enjoys spendint it outdoors with this friends and peers. Being able to make plans ahead of time with his hecktic schedule is important to him.

Scenerios

  • I want to go for a hike, will the weather be good?
  • I'd like to learn more about my town, what information is available.
  • I have some free time and would like to have cook out with some friends. Is it going to be windy?
  • My yard needs some tender loving care. Is this Saturday going to be good yard working weather?
  • My kids want to go to the park and play tomorrow, will it be good weather?

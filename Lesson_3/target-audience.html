Target-Audience

The target audience is perminent residents and visitors interested in information specific to each town. This includes single and married adults, teenagers and college students. Additionally, those that want to plan their outdoor activities and need weather infromation are also a releviant audience.

Persona

The Bronsons

The Bronsons are a small young family that enjoy bonding as a family outdoors with their dog rimshot. They enjoy planning outdoor events, so they need a place that can give them weather information specific to Preston.

Roger Young

Roger is a college student in Rexburg Idaho. Most of his time is spent indoors studying. Howeever, when he has spare time he enjoys spendint it outdoors with this friends and peers. Being able to make plans ahead of time with his hecktic schedule is important to him.

White Space & Clean Design

Native-Instruments.com

The same amount of whitespace isn't used for each area. It uses enough whitespace to create distinctive sections. This illistrates that the amount used needs to be considered for each element to find the right balance.

Contrast

Playstation.com

The main section has really dark colors, but using white letters allows the use of dark colors and be readable. Also, the white "Watch now" button is another good example of good contrast.

Visual Hierarchy

Izotope.com

The big white lettering draws the viewer in first. It is the most important part of the page, so it is the biggest. Next, the images connected to the product are followed in the hierarchy. Next, the button followed by the smaller writing and navigation complete the hierarchy structure.

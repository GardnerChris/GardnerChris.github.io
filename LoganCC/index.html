Logan Valley CC Mission

  • The purpose of Cache Valley CC, is to strengthen business connections, increase revenue, expand clientele, and increase the social capital of Cache Valley residents.
  • For businesses, this is a platform that allows them to advertise important consumer information like product updates, company announcements, and events. This provides a place where businesses and customers can interact while building customer loyalty.
  • For consumers, this is an informational hub geared towards learning about new and current businesses. A list of several influential businesses in Cache Valley provides a solid consumer database for each consumer to have a place where their needs can be met.
  • For all residents, this provides a place to learn historical information about Cache Valley, see upcoming events, and strengthen ties to the communities. Current weather and weather forecasts will be provided to address a range of needs and interests of each resident.
  • For people new to Cache Valley CC, a portal where new members can review plans, understand services, and join a plan based on their needs.

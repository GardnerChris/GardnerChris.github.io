Celebrating the Flag

The Preston Elk Lodge celebrated and honered the American flag on the steps of the city courthouse.Josh Anderson, president of the lodge says "We hold our flag, our veterans and our United States close to our hearts."

The celebration consisted of a history of the flag from its humble beginnings to how we know it today. This celebration has a rich history going back to the turn of the centery. However, it wasn't until after World War II that flag day was offically regognized on June 14.

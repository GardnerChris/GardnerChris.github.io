What can you expect to find here?

On-Point weather is your portal for up-to-date weather of Southern Idaho. The mission of this site is to give you the information you need to proactively plan your outdoor adventures, vacations, and family events with peace of mind. Current weather conditions, a 5-day forecast, and relevant town information can be found on each page.

Who is this site for?

This page is for everyone that lives in southern Idaho or those that want to visit these beautiful towns. Whether you are planning a picnic with your kids, a fishing trip with your friends, or simply want to know if you will have good weather to catch up on outdoor gardening, this is the place for you.

We are glad you found your way to On-Point Weather, and hope you enjoy your stay!
